Yves' Weather Station Programme (1)

This is the first time that I've tried to build a weather station. The purpose of this is that I would like predict the weather without using the central Weather Bureau's data.

There are 2 sensors I use to predict the weather. One is used to record the temperature, the other is used to record the humidity. Every 5 minutes, they will send the data directly to my Google Form, which allows me to read and analyze the data.
